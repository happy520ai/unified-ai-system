# Phase1476A Concept Field Critical Audit

## Audit Verdict

- strategicInspiration=true
- crossEraEmpiricalProof=false
- llmReplacement=false
- agiClaimAllowed=false
- trillionModelSurpassClaimAllowed=false
- experimentalSubKernelOnly=true

## Adoptable Structure

- Treat concept activation as a local scoring field, not as a replacement for language-model reasoning.
- Keep positive, negative, and neutral sources explicit so routing, evidence replay, and safety review can inspect why a concept was activated or suppressed.
- Use bounded iteration and deterministic seeds so dry-run output is repeatable.
- Use the kernel as a benchmark scaffold for route affinity, evidence coherence, surprise, and risk signals.

## Rejected Claims

- No cross-era empirical proof is accepted.
- No AGI claim is allowed.
- No claim that this surpasses trillion-parameter models is allowed.
- No claim that synthetic vector behavior validates real semantic understanding is allowed.
- No claim that the kernel can replace provider models, LLMs, or the default gateway runtime is allowed.

## Engineering Boundary

The concept field kernel is an experimental Taiji / Beidou sub-kernel. It can produce synthetic dry-run readouts and evidence, but it cannot enter `/chat`, `/chat-gateway/execute`, provider runtime, deployment, release, or production readiness claims in Phase1476.
