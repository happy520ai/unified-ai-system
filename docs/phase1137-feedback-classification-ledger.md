## Categories

- comprehension: user does not understand purpose, CTA, mode, or next step.
- safety-anxiety: user worries that preview may call a model, read secrets, or deploy.
- interaction: click, keyboard, drawer, or focus issue.
- responsive: layout problem at desktop, narrow desktop, or tablet-like width.
- copy: confusing wording.
- visual: too dense, noisy, or hard to scan.
- blocker: prevents local trial completion.

## Severity

- P0: safety boundary failure, secret exposure, unexpected execution, or data loss.
- P1: user cannot complete preview flow.
- P2: serious confusion but workaround exists.
- P3: minor friction.
- ordinary: polish or wording preference.
