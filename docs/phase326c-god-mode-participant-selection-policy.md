# Phase326C God Mode Participant Selection Policy

## Selection Rules

- `userConfiguredOnly`
- `userCredentialRequired`
- `providerGovernanceApproved`
- `modelAvailabilityChecked`
- `noDisabledProvider`
- `noUnverifiedModel`
- `noFailedOrHighRiskModel`
- `capabilityDiversityPreferred`
- `roleDiversityPreferred`

## Roles

- `primary_responder`
- `critic`
- `fact_checker`
- `domain_specialist`
- `safety_guard`
- `supervisor`

## Participant Count

- `minimumParticipants: 2`
- `recommendedParticipants: 3-5`
- `maximumParticipants`: governed by user policy and cost policy

## Adjudication Principle

- God Mode is not simple voting
- God Mode is not simple fan-out
- Supervisor must not blindly follow the majority
- disagreement, uncertainty, and conflict resolution must remain visible
